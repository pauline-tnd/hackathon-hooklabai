# Getting Started (Base Mini App)

### Open the mini app

1. Open the **Base app**.
2. Launch the **HookLab mini app**.

You land on a simple landing screen.

### Start and connect your wallet

1. Tap **Get started**.
2. Connect your **Base wallet**.

We use your wallet address to:

* Track your credit balance.
* Attribute generated content to your session.
* Process pay-per-generation purchases.

{% hint style="info" %}
First-time users get **5 free credits** after wallet connect.
{% endhint %}
